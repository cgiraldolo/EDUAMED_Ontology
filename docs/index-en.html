Ontology of the european catalogue of high-risk medical devices and in-vitro diagnostics devices.: Description back to ToC

This ontology semantically models the catalogue of high-risk medical devices and in-vitro diagnostic devices of the EUDAMED european database. It includes devices, manufacturers, identified risks, incidents and corrective safety actions.
